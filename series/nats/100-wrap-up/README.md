# 100 — Course Wrap-up & Certification

> **Type:** Explanation  
> **Phase:** Advanced & Real-World

## Congratulations! 🎉

You've completed the **100-Lesson NATS Series**. You've gone from the very basics of Pub/Sub to building global, multi-tenant, resilient event-driven architectures.

## What you've learned:
- **Foundations:** Core NATS protocol, subjects, and patterns.
- **JetStream:** Durable persistence, streams, and consumers.
- **Architecture:** CQRS, Saga patterns, and service discovery.
- **Operations:** Clustering, security, monitoring, and scaling.
- **Advanced:** Edge computing, Web browser connectivity, and migration strategies.

## Continuing your Journey

1. **The NATS CLI:** Keep it in your terminal. It's the best way to explore and debug any NATS cluster.
2. **Community:** Join the [NATS Slack](https://slack.nats.io/) to ask questions and share your projects.
3. **Open Source:** NATS is open source. Check out the [NATS Server GitHub](https://github.com/nats-io/nats-server) and contribute!

## Final Challenge
Build a small, end-to-end "Live Auction" system:
- **Backend:** Go microservices for bidding and inventory.
- **Frontend:** React + WebSockets for live price updates.
- **Persistence:** JetStream for the audit log.
- **Security:** NKey authentication.

## Graduation
You are now a **NATS Practitioner**. The world of distributed systems is now yours to connect.
