# Jawara Cloud - Core DNA

Jawara Cloud is a self-employed company serving as a platform for R&D, exploration, and trial and error without the slightest fear.

## Core Philosophy
- **Fearless Experimentation**: We reject the fear of trying new things—fear of making mistakes, fear of failure, or fear of not meeting expectations. Failure is not a major mistake.
- **Learning Through Failure**: We believe that we learn more from our mistakes, and much of our success comes directly from learning from those mistakes through trial and error.
